# Reference Persona: The Experienced Player

**Name:** Priya "Static" Vasquez
**Role:** Player
**Experience:** 6+ years playing SR5 across multiple campaigns. Has played Street Samurai, Face, Decker, and Adept archetypes.

## Background

Priya thinks in dice pools. She calculates her shooting pool, defense pool, and social pool before she's chosen her character's name. She expects a digital tool to keep up with her mechanical thinking. She is not looking for a video game — she wants a digital version of the tabletop experience that enforces the rules she already knows.

## Primary Goals

- Build valid characters that match her intended mechanical concept
- See dice pools calculated live as she allocates attributes and skills
- Understand the downstream effect of every choice before committing (Essence loss -> Magic cap, quality effects on pools)
- Track damage, ammo, Edge, and gear state during play without a separate spreadsheet
- Advance her character between sessions with Karma and nuyen using correctly enforced costs
- Never have to silently build an invalid character — she wants clear blocking with an explanation

## Key Pain Points

- Any tool that does not show calculated dice pools
- Gear management that doesn't distinguish between what's in hand, in a bag, and stored at home
- No live preview of a choice's downstream effects before committing
- Character state not persisting reliably between sessions
- Karma advancement that allows illegal purchases or calculates costs incorrectly

## Most Frequent Workflows

1. Character creation: New runner from scratch via Priority selection
2. Character creation: Iterating — swap a priority, observe all downstream changes live
3. Between sessions: Spend Karma on skill raises, qualities, or initiation
4. Between sessions: Purchase new gear, update lifestyle
5. During session: Track Physical and Stun damage
6. During session: Track ammo, grenades, Edge spent
7. During session: Quick-reference a skill's linked attribute or a quality's effect

## Definition of Success

Priya builds her Adept in under 30 minutes. Power Point allocation is reflected in dice pools immediately. Her GM approves the sheet on the spot because it's valid. She tracks the entire session — damage, Edge, ammo — without leaving the tool.

## SR5-Specific Expectations

- Agility governs most combat skills; attribute linkages must be correct
- Cyberware Essence costs are decimal-precise and only rounded at the total
- Karma costs for skill raises follow the rating x multiplier formula exactly
- Specializations grant exactly +2 dice for the specific application
- Edge is tracked as both a maximum stat and a current resource independently
- Augmented attribute values are visually distinguished from natural values (relevant to augmentation caps)
