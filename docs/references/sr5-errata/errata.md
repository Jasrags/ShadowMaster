# SR5 Core Rulebook Errata

**Source:** Shadowrun Fifth Edition Errata (02/09/2014)
**PDF Pages:** 1–4

---

## Overview

Official corrections and clarifications to the Shadowrun 5th Edition Core Rulebook. Covers character creation, skills, combat, magic, matrix, gear, and critter stat blocks.

---

## Character Creation

### Troll Lifestyle Costs (p. 65)

- Trolls receive Thermographic Vision, +1 Reach, and Dermal Armor
- **Disadvantage:** Lifestyle costs doubled to reflect adapting gear (including cyberware and bioware) to meet massive physical requirements

### Dwarf Racial Bonuses (p. 66, Metatype Attribute Table)

- "Thermographic Vision" should be added as a racial bonus for dwarves

### Mystic Adept Power Points (p. 71)

- Karma cost for Power Points changed from **2 Karma to 5 Karma per full Power Point**
- Also affects the fifth bullet point under **Mystic Adepts** on p. 69

### Troll and Dwarf Gear Costs (p. 94, Spend Your Resources, Paragraph 3)

- Remove paragraph about racial gear cost modifiers for dwarves and trolls
- Replace with: "If the player finds that he or she has more than the 5,000 nuyen that can be saved (or even if he or she hasn't), there are a few essential pieces of gear to consider when building a shadowrunner. You'll want a commlink (p. 438) to stay in touch and to keep your gear relatively safe from hackers. A fake SIN (p. 442), along with some fake licenses, will help smooth dealing with law enforcement or even simple purchases like buying a bus ticket or covering a bar tab. For more ideas, see the Gear Checklist sidebar."

### Sample Character Changes

- **Street Samurai:** Add contact: Fixer (Connection 4, Loyalty 2)
- **Street Shaman:** Social limit changed from 7 to **8**
- **Combat Mage:** Physical limit changed from 6 to **5**
- **Face:** Physical limit changed from 3 to **4**, Mental limit changed from 5 to **6**
- **Tank:** Physical limit changed from 10 to **9 (11)**
- **Decker:** Mental limit changed from 6 (7) to **7**
- **Drone Rigger:** Physical limit changed from 6 to **5 (6)**, Mental limit changed from 5 to **6**
- **Sprawl Ganger:** Limits changed from Physical 8, Mental 4, Social 5 to **Physical 9, Mental 5, Social 6**
- **Bounty Hunter:** Physical limit changed from 7 to **9**, Social limit changed from 4 to **5**

## Skills

### Agility Skill List (p. 90)

- Remove "Herding" and "Lockpicking" — these are not skills in SR5

### Rappelling Distance (p. 134, Climbing Table)

- Movement Distance column for rappelling changed from **2 meters to 20 meters + 1 meter per hit**

### Target Roll on Intimidation Tests (p. 141, Social Skill Tests Table)

- Line 4: Change target test from "Intimidation + Willpower [Social]" to **"Charisma + Willpower"**

### Magical Skills Clarification (p. 142)

- Change: "In order to acquire magic-specific skills, characters must have a quality that provides a Magic rating."
- To: "In order to acquire magic-specific skills, characters must be an Aspected Magician, Magician, or Mystic Adept."

### Biotech Skill Group (p. 153)

- Add **Biotechnology** to the Biotech skill group

## Combat

### Roll Initiative Term Correction (p. 158)

- Change "Action Phase" to **"Combat Turn"** in the second sentence

### Block & Parry Clarification (p. 168, Interrupt Actions)

- Remove from Block description: "Bonus die, such as die from a relevant weapon foci, can be added to this test."
- Add that sentence to **Parry** description instead

### Fire Example Correction (p. 172, Example, Paragraph 3)

- Add at beginning of paragraph: "The next Combat Turn begins, and the fire's DV increases to 4P."

### Recoil Clarification (p. 175, Progressive Recoil, Paragraph 1)

- Recoil penalties are cumulative over every Action Phase and Combat Turn unless the character takes, or is forced into, a **Simple or Complex Action** other than shooting
- (Was incorrectly "an action other than shooting")

### Multiple Attack and Edge Clarification (p. 196, Paragraph 2)

- Change: "Edge spent on this test adds into the pool before it is divided."
- To: **"Dice gained by spending Edge applies before the pool is split, while dice from both pools can be re-rolled with a single use of Edge."**

### Damaging Vehicle Passengers Correction (p. 205, Paragraph 1)

- Exceptions that affect both passengers and vehicles: ramming, **suppressive fire**, and area-effect weapon attacks like grenades and rockets
- (Added "suppressive fire" to the list)

## Matrix

### Matrix Actions Correction (p. 244, Matrix Actions by Limit Table)

- Remove **Crack File** from the Sleaze section of the table

### IC and Marks Clarification (p. 247, Intrusion Countermeasures, Paragraph 2)

- Change: "The IC in a host and the host itself share marks, so if one IC program is slapped with a mark, they all get one, as does the host itself."
- To: **"The IC in a host and the host itself share marks, so if one IC program marks, they all do, and so does the host itself."**

### Resonance and Essence Clarification (p. 250, Resonance, Paragraph 2)

- Change: "Whenever you lose Essence (after character generation), you lose an equal amount of Resonance, rounded up."
- To: **"For every point (or fraction thereof) of Essence lost, both your current Resonance Attribute and your maximum Resonance Rating are reduced by one."**

### Registering Sprite Limit (p. 256, Paragraph 2)

- Add after "Your registered sprite will stay with you as long as it still owes you at least one task":
- **"You can register a number of sprites equal to or less than your Logic attribute."**

## Magic

### Power Points and Magic Clarification (p. 279)

- Change: "You get a free Power Point whenever you increase your Magic attribute, and you can gain a Power Point through Initiation (p. 324) instead of gaining a metamagic."
- To: **"If you're an adept, you get a free Power Point whenever you increase your Magic attribute (though this doesn't apply to mystic adepts, and you can gain a Power Point through Initiation (p. 324) instead of gaining a metamagic."**

### Combat Sense Correction (p. 286)

- Change characteristic from "(Active, Psychic)" to **"(Passive, Psychic)"**

### Resist Pain Drain Correction (p. 289)

- Change drain value from "(Damage Value) – 6" to **"F – 4"**

### Homunculus Movement Correction (p. 298, Stat Block)

- Change movement from "15/30" to **"x2/x4/+1"**

### Preparations and Lynchpins Clarification (p. 305, Paragraph 1)

- Change: "When Potency reaches 0 it loses its magical nature and the spell is lost."
- To: **"When Potency reaches 0, or the lynchpin is broken, it loses its magical nature and the spell is lost."**

### Astral Intersection Test Correction (p. 316, Paragraph 2)

- Change: "Magic + Charisma [Astral]" to **"Magic + Charisma"** (no limit — two-attribute tests don't use limits)
- Change: "Force x 2 [Force]" to **"Force x 2"**

### Deactivating Focus Clarification (p. 318, Foci, Paragraph 3)

- Change: "You can deactivate a focus at any time."
- To: **"You can deactivate a focus at any time with a Free Action."**

## Implementation Notes

- Errata corrections should be treated as authoritative — they override corresponding core rulebook text
- Many corrections fix **sample character stat blocks** — if importing archetypes, use corrected values
- The Power Points cost change (2→5 Karma) is a significant balance change affecting mystic adept builds
- The "no racial gear modifiers" change simplifies character creation for trolls/dwarves
- Several skill renames (Infiltration→Sneaking, Data Search removed, Herding/Lockpicking removed) reflect SR5's skill consolidation
- Block vs Parry distinction for weapon foci bonus dice is mechanically important for combat
- Sprite registration limit (Logic attribute) was missing from core — critical for technomancer validation
