# Positive Metagenic Qualities

**Source:** Run Faster, p.111-118
**PDF Pages:** 113-120

---

## Overview

Positive Metagenic Qualities are SURGE-related traits that provide beneficial mutations. Characters spend metagenic Karma on these qualities during changeling character creation, balanced against Negative Metagenic Qualities. The class of SURGE determines the available metagenic Karma budget.

---

## Rules

### 360-Degree Eyesight (4 Karma)

Repositioned or compound eyes grant 360-degree field of vision. +1 dice pool modifier on vision-based Perception Tests and Surprise Tests. -1 dice pool modifier to any tests while in motion (may negate other modifiers). -1 dice pool modifier on Ranged Attack Tests at distances greater than 10 meters. Modifiers are cumulative. Cannot be augmented; incompatible with eye replacement. Characters normally incur social modifiers (see Freaks sidebar, p. 123).

### Animal Pelage (3 to 5 Karma)

Character grows unusual fur or pelage with both cosmetic and environmental function. Counts as Unusual Hair (p. 122) for social effects. Sub-types:

- **Camo Fur (3 Karma):** Dark or patterned fur grants +1 dice pool modifier to Sneaking Tests in decreased light or pattern-appropriate environments. Incompatible with other natural/artificial hair or skin modifications.
- **Insulating Pelt (4 Karma):** Thick fur covering entire body. +2 dice pool modifier to Survival Tests in frigid environments (Arctic/Subarctic). +4 Armor against Cold-based attacks. -2 dice pool modifier to Survival Tests in extreme heat. Clothing/armor can be worn over it. Incompatible with other natural/artificial hair or skin modifications.
- **Quills (5 Karma):** Keratin plates resembling porcupine quills (2-4 cm, sharp). Defensive: when grappled, attacker makes Damage Resistance Test vs DV (STR + 1)P, AP +1, using higher Strength of the pair. Offensive: can wield as Exotic Melee Weapon (Quills) with DV (STR + 1)P, Reach --, AP +1. Arms or legs must be exposed. Incompatible with other natural/artificial hair or skin modifications.

### Arcane Arrester (10 Karma per level, max 2)

Adds Level x 2 dice to resistance tests against any spell (including critter Innate Spells). If the spell is not normally resisted (e.g., Invisibility), the character still rolls their Arcane Arrester dice pool of 2 or 4 dice and reduces the caster's Spellcasting Test hits by any hits rolled. Cannot be combined with Magic Resistance (p. 76, SR5). Can only be taken by characters with a Magic attribute. Affects spells they cast on themselves.

> **Ambiguity:** The text says "reduces the hits rolled on the Spellcasting Test by any hits they roll" -- this means the character's Arcane Arrester hits subtract from the caster's hits, not a separate resistance test.

### Balance Receptor (6 Karma)

Statocyst-like balance organ augments balance. +1 dice pool modifier to all tests involving balance: Agility-based Tests to maintain balance, Climbing Tests, Jumping Tests, and all Gymnastics Tests. Incompatible with cyberears and the balance augmenter augmentation.

### Beak (3 or 5 Karma)

Face replaced by a beak (varies in size/shape). Cannot chew; swallows food whole (gizzard processes it). Expands edible substances by 10%, reducing Lifestyle costs by 10%. +1 dice pool modifier on tests to resist ingested toxins (see Toxins, p. 408, SR5). Characters with Beak (including Raptor Beak) suffer Freaks social modifiers (p. 123).

- **Raptor Beak (5 Karma):** Combat-capable. Unarmed Combat skill, DV (STR + 2)P, Reach -1, AP -2.

### Bicardiac (4 Karma)

Second heart in right torso augments cardiovascular system. Double the fatigue damage interval for strenuous activities (running, sprinting, hot/cold environments). See Fatigue Damage (p. 172, SR5). Compatible with synthacardium; only the primary heart can be augmented.

### Biosonar (4 Karma)

Biological echolocation (bat/dolphin-like). Free Action (Speak) to emit; creates image out to 50 meters usable for Attack or vision-based Perception Tests. Cannot perceive changes between emissions but can detect changes through regular Perception Tests. Ear shape changes provide +1 dice pool modifier to audio-based Perception Tests and +1 DV modifier to all sonic-based attacks against the character.

### Bone Spikes (5 Karma)

Bony protrusions through skin. Cannot wear standard armor or restrictive clothing; custom armor/clothing costs +50% of base cost. Increases danger in melee: unarmed melee damage is (STR + 2)P.

### Broadened Auditory Spectrum (4 Karma)

Choose Ultrasound or Infrasound hearing at purchase. Allows Perception Test to detect communication/sounds in chosen frequency band. +1 to limit of Perception Tests involving sound. May be taken twice (once per frequency). Incompatible with any earware (p. 453, SR5).

### Camouflage (4 or 8 Karma)

Skin/hair changes color to match background (chameleon-like). Incompatible with any other dermal modification. Characters suffer Freaks social modifiers (p. 123).

- **Basic Camouflage (4 Karma):** Changes color after 1 minute of exposure to a background with sharp contrasts. Retains color up to 4 hours; fades in 10 minutes if background changes. While active, increase threshold to see character by 2 (must be standing still, no clothing).
- **Dynamic Coloration (8 Karma):** Changes color slowly with background, even while moving. -2 to visual perception test to see character while moving, -4 if stationary. Character cannot be wearing clothing.

### Celerity (6 Karma)

Strengthened running musculature. Walking rate = Agility x 3, Running rate = Agility x 6, Sprint bonus = +1m/turn. Incompatible with any leg- or muscle-altering system (cyberlimbs, muscle replacement, muscle toner, muscle augmentation) and Satyr Legs.

### Claws (3, 5, or 7 Karma)

Fingernails/toenails harden into claws. Sub-types:

- **Digging Claws (3 Karma):** +2 dice pool modifier to digging/moving earth tests. Combat: Unarmed Combat, DV (STR)P, Reach --, AP +1. Incompatible with cyberweapon or limb replacement cyberware.
- **Razor Claws (5 Karma):** Unarmed Combat, DV (STR + 1)P, AP -1.
- **Retractable Claws (7 Karma):** Same as Razor Claws but can be retracted and hidden. Digging claws cannot be retracted.

### Climate Adaptation (4 Karma)

Choose Desert or Arctic climate at purchase. +1 dice pool modifier to Survival Tests (p. 136, SR5) and resisting fatigue damage (p. 172, SR5) in chosen environment. -1 dice pool modifier in the opposite environment. Incompatible with dermal plating, any skin modifications, or a suprathyroid gland.

### Corrosive Spit (5 Karma)

Salivary glands produce acid. Ranged Attack using Exotic Ranged Weapon (Corrosive Spit) + Agility [Physical], range = Body meters. Causes Acid damage (p. 170, SR5), DV 6P. Can be used once every 30 minutes.

### Dermal Alteration (3 to 15 Karma)

Skin with unique appearance and properties. Incompatible with any other natural or artificial skin modification. Sub-types:

- **Bark Skin (5 Karma):** Woven cellulose fibers; +2 armor, cumulative with worn armor.
- **Blubber (3 Karma):** Thick vascularized fat layer; +2 armor when resisting Cold damage, -2 dice pool modifier to Perception Tests involving cold.
- **Dragon Skin (3 Karma):** Scaly hide; +2 armor when resisting Fire damage, -2 dice pool modifier to Perception Tests involving heat.
- **Granite Shell (15 Karma):** Massive calcite formations; +4 armor functioning as Hardened Armor (p. 397, SR5). Not fully stackable with worn armor -- does not stack for Damage Resistance Tests but still provides 2 automatic hits from Hardened Armor.
- **Rhino Hide (7 Karma):** Hard, thick, leathery skin; +3 armor, cumulative with worn armor.

> **Ambiguity:** Granite Shell's interaction with worn armor is complex. It functions as Hardened Armor but "does not stack with worn armor" for Damage Resistance Tests while still providing the 2 automatic hits. Implementation should treat it as Hardened Armor that replaces (rather than stacks with) worn armor for the resistance roll.

### Dermal Deposits (3 Karma)

Tough dermal deposits under/on skin. +1 natural armor, cumulative with worn armor. If character is a troll, the deposits stack with the troll's existing +1 armor (total +2 from natural armor).

### Defensive Secretion (4 Karma)

Skin exudes defensive liquid when agitated/endangered (acidic or burning). Any person touching bare unprotected skin of the agitated character suffers -1 dice pool modifier to all tests for 24 hours or until treated with Medicine + Logic [Mental] (2) Test. Does not work against spirits.

### Electroception (4 or 7 Karma)

Sense electricity and electrical field changes. Observe in Detail Simple Action: Perception + Intuition [Mental] Test. Range = Essence meters. Results determined by Electroception Table.

- **Electrosense (4 Karma):** Detect electromagnetic fields (like fish/platypuses). Devices must require electrical energy to be detected.
- **Technosense (7 Karma):** Sense higher energies of wireless technology (like technomancers). Can sense wireless signals but cannot manipulate them.
  Noise modifiers affect the Perception Test.

> **Cross-reference:** Electroception Table on p. 114 defines what information is revealed at each hit threshold (1-5+ hits) for both Electrosense and Technosense. See companion JSON for full table.

### Elongated Limbs (4 Karma)

Arms and legs elongated, granting Reach +1 (cumulative with existing Reach). Must buy special clothing/armor at +10% cost. Ill-fitting armor rating reduced by 10% (round up).

### Fangs (4 Karma)

Enlarged canine teeth. Unarmed Combat skill, DV (STR + 1)P, Reach -1, AP --.

### Frog Tongue (4 Karma)

Long adhesive tongue extended by muscular trigger (spring-loaded). Can snatch objects weighing less than unaugmented Strength x 100 grams. Tongue sticks until manually removed. If character has Natural Venom (p. 117), tongue can deliver touch-only melee attack (p. 187, SR5) using Exotic Melee Weapon (Frog Tongue).

### Functional Tail (4 to 7 Karma)

Fully developed tail (scaly, hairy, or hairless). Clothing must accommodate it; sitting for long periods causes -1 dice pool modifier to all actions. Incompatible with any other tail modification or quality. Characters suffer Freaks social modifiers (p. 123). Sub-types:

- **Balance (6 Karma):** 1-2 meters long, not under conscious control. +1 dice pool modifier to Balance, Climbing, Gymnastics, and Jumping-related tests.
- **Paddle (4 Karma):** Broad beaver-like tail. +2 dice pool modifier to Swimming Tests.
- **Prehensile (7 Karma):** Conscious manipulation like an extra limb. Effective Strength = half unaugmented Strength (round down). Can hold character's body weight. -4 dice pool modifier to fine manipulation with tail. No extra attack.
- **Thagomizer (5 Karma):** Muscled prehensile tail ending in dermal spikes. Exotic Melee Weapon (Thagomizer), DV (STR + 3)P, Reach 1, AP -1.

### Gills (4 or 6 Karma)

Amphibian-like gills (neck, pectoral region, or armpits). Choose Air or Aqua gills at purchase.

- **4 Karma:** Gills aren't perfect. Fatigue Test durations halved in the non-native environment (air for aqua gills, water for air gills).
- **6 Karma:** Gills function equally in either environment with no penalty.

### Glamour (12 Karma)

Paranaturally gifted with unearthly grace. +2 to Social Limit. +1 dice pool modifier to all Social Skill Tests except Intimidation. Character is particularly memorable; suffers Distinctive Style effects (p. 80, SR5).

### Goring Horns (5 Karma)

Horn(s) on head (ram, antler, devil, bone crest, etc.). Exotic Melee Weapon (Horns), DV (STR + 2)P, Reach --, AP -1.

### Greasy Skin (3 Karma)

Sweat glands produce greasy lubricant under stress. -2 dice pool modifier to Subduing melee attacks against the character (more than half body exposed). Incompatible with extensive body hair or skin modifications/qualities.

### Keen-Eared (3 Karma)

Extraordinarily keen hearing from overly large ears. +1 dice pool modifier to audio-based Perception Tests.

### Larger Tusks (5 Karma)

Bigger tusk variants (elephant, warthog, walrus). Unarmed Combat, DV (STR + 2)P, Reach --, AP --. Incompatible with mouth implants and modifications (except Proboscis, p. 117). Characters suffer Freaks social modifiers (p. 123).

### Low-Light Vision (2 or 4 Karma)

Natural low-light vision (see normally down to starlight levels). Cannot be combined with eye replacement.

- **2 Karma:** Reflective feline eyes with slit irises. Affect social interactions (Freaks sidebar, p. 123).
- **4 Karma:** Normal eyes resulting from metagenic background (elf/ork eyes). No social penalty.

### Magic Sense (7 Karma)

Detect magic in vicinity. Functions similar to Detect Magic spell (p. 287, SR5) but uses Intuition + Willpower [Astral] instead of Spellcasting + Magic [Force]. Range = Magic x 5 meters (minimum 5 meters, including characters with no Magic Rating).

### Magnetoception (4 Karma)

Sense changes in nearby magnetic fields via elevated magnetite in ethmoid bone. Unerring ability to locate magnetic north: +1 dice pool bonus to Navigation Tests. Can detect electronics, power supplies, and magnetic anomaly detectors via Perception + Intuition [Mental] vs Object Resistance Table (p. 293, SR5) within 5 meters.

### Marsupial Pouch (3 Karma)

Pocket-like pouch on chest or abdomen. Functions as Smuggling Compartment with Concealability modifier of -6 (p. 455, SR5).

### Metagenic (Attribute) Improvement (15 Karma)

Genetic expression enhancing one Mental or Physical attribute. Minimum and maximum values for the chosen attribute (per Metatype Attribute Table, p. 66, SR5) are both raised by 1. May only be taken once per attribute. Cumulative with Exceptional Attribute quality (p. 72, SR5).

### Metahuman Traits (2 Karma)

Cosmetic metahuman traits (elongated ears, dwarfism, gigantism, ork tusks). +1 dice pool modifier when disguising as the resembled metatype, similar to Elf or Ork Poser. Only available to human characters.

### Monkey Paws (4 Karma)

Monkey/chameleon-like gripping feet with prehensile toes. +2 dice pool modifier to Gymnastics, Climbing, and Movement in Zero Gravity (p. 163, Run & Gun) tests when barefoot. If wearing shoes that accommodate the feet, modifier reduced to +1.

### Nasty Vibe (5 Karma)

Character radiates an indefinable bad vibe (menace, oppression). +2 dice pool modifier to all Intimidation Tests and any attempt to instill fear in a sapient target.

### Natural Venom (3 to 20 Karma)

Body produces animal toxin (character is immune). Toxin found on Animal Toxins Table. Delivery vectors: Inhalation (3 Karma), Contact (5 Karma), Injected (0 Karma, requires Fangs p. 115). Total Karma cost = Vector cost + Severity cost (from Animal Toxins Table). Incompatible with Corrosive Spit.

> **Cross-reference:** Animal Toxins Table on p. 117 defines severity levels (Mild/Moderate/Serious/Deadly) with Karma costs (3/5/10/15), Speed, Power, Effect, and Penetration. See companion JSON for full table.

### Ogre Stomach (8 Karma)

Can digest unusual substances (raw meat, cellulose, grass). Lifestyle costs reduced by 20%. +2 dice pool modifier on Toxin Resistance Tests to resist ingested toxins (p. 408, SR5).

### Photometabolism (4 Karma)

Chloroplast-like organ (skin or grasslike body hair) supplements energy via photosynthesis. Reduces Lifestyle costs by 10%. Does not replace diet. Character feels uncomfortable at night/in shade: -1 dice pool penalty on all Social Tests. Incompatible with Nocturnal (p. 121) and all skin/hair modifications.

### Proboscis (5 Karma)

Prehensile nose/elephantine trunk. Exotic Melee Weapon (Trunk), DV (STR - 1)P, Reach --, AP +1. -2 dice pool modifier to fine manipulation with trunk. Effective Strength = unaugmented Strength. No extra attack. Characters suffer Freaks social modifiers (p. 123).

### Satyr Legs (10 Karma)

Quadruped-shaped hind legs (cloven hooves, kangaroo, hare, etc.). Running rate = Agility x 6, Sprint bonus = +1m/turn. +2 Strength modifier to damage of kicking attacks. Characters suffer Freaks social modifiers (p. 123). Incompatible with Celerity.

### Setae (4 Karma)

Gecko-like hairlike structures on palms. Character always uses assisted climbing rates (p. 134, SR5) as long as hands are exposed.

### Shiva Arms (8 Karma per pair)

Additional pair of arms from modified shoulder joints. May hold/carry double normal objects. Arms move independently but character still has one dominant hand unless they have Ambidextrous (p. 71, SR5). Can wield multiple weapons (firearms and/or melee); off-hand penalties apply. Can use Multiple Attacks Free Action (p. 164, SR5) with firearms in extra hands (must split dice pool). May be taken twice (max 6 arms total). Characters suffer Freaks social modifiers (p. 123).

### Thermal Sensitivity (4 Karma)

Infrared-sensitive receptors near olfactory organs detect radiated heat. Non-visual Perception Test to detect heat sources (bodies, electronics, etc.) within 10 meters. Adjustable for warm environments, lower heat outputs, or heat pollution. Not fooled by Invisibility or Silence spells. Reduces Visibility and Light/Glare modifiers by one level when using thermal sensitivity in combat.

### Thermographic Vision (3 Karma)

Natural thermographic vision identical to dwarf/troll vision.

### Thorns (2 Karma)

1 cm hard sharp protrusions through skin (modified keratin). +1 DV to any Unarmed Combat attack. Constant discomfort: -1 to all Physical Tests. Must have accommodating clothing; customization increases gear cost by 20-100%.

### Underwater Vision (3 Karma)

Additional adapted membrane on eyes enables unrestricted vision in/out of water. Can use underwater visibility modifiers (p. 157, Run & Gun) as if wearing goggles/mask.

### Vomeronasal Organ (3 Karma)

Enhanced olfactory organelles augment smell. Can identify individuals by scent and detect basic emotions (fear, anger, lust). +2 dice pool modifier to Perception Tests based on smell. +1 dice pool modifier to all Social Tests when character can smell target. Strong odors cause -1 dice pool modifier to all tests (respirator helps). Overwhelming odors impose -1 to all tests. Tailored pheromones provide twice normal bonus against this character. If character has adrenaline pump (p. 459, SR5), others' fear/anger/lust scents may trigger the pump (Composure (2) Test, p. 152, SR5).

### Webbed Digits (4 Karma)

Webbed fingers and toes. +2 dice pool modifier to all Swimming Tests. -1 dice pool modifier to fine manipulations using webbed digits.

## Implementation Notes

- Many qualities reference the **Freaks sidebar (p. 123)**: every quality referencing Freaks imposes -1 dice pool modifier to all Social tests per quality taken, and Social Limit decreases by 1 for every 3 such qualities.
- **Incompatibility tracking** is critical -- many qualities conflict with cyberware, bioware, or other metagenic qualities. Implement as a validation layer during character creation.
- **Exotic Melee/Ranged Weapons** require the corresponding skill (Exotic Melee Weapon (Quills), Exotic Ranged Weapon (Corrosive Spit), etc.). Each is a separate skill specialization.
- **Natural Venom** Karma cost is calculated: delivery vector cost + toxin severity cost. This needs a dynamic cost calculator, not a static value.
- **Dermal Alteration** and **Animal Pelage** are umbrella qualities with mutually exclusive sub-types; only one sub-type can be selected.
- Qualities with variable costs (e.g., "3 or 5 Karma", "4 to 7 Karma") indicate sub-type selection, not a sliding scale.
- **Low-Light Vision** at 2 Karma triggers Freaks social modifiers; at 4 Karma it does not. This is a cost-vs-penalty trade-off.
