# Creating a Shadowrunner

**Source:** SR5 Core Rulebook, p.62–107
**PDF Pages:** 64–109

---

## Overview

Complete character creation rules for Shadowrun 5th Edition using the Priority System. Characters are built in 9 steps: choose concept, assign priorities (Metatype, Attributes, Magic/Resonance, Skills, Resources), purchase qualities, buy skills, spend resources on gear, spend leftover Karma, calculate derived stats, and finalize. All characters start with 25 Karma. Three gameplay levels are supported: Street, Standard (default), and Prime Runner.

---

## Rules

### Step 1: Choose Concept

Pick a character archetype/concept. Common types: Face, Spellcaster, Decker, Technomancer, Rigger, Street Samurai. This is purely narrative — no mechanical impact.

### Step 2: Choose Metatype (Priority System)

The Priority System has 5 columns (Metatype, Attributes, Magic/Resonance, Skills, Resources) and 5 rows (A–E). Each row may only be assigned once — no duplicates.

- **Metatype column** determines available metatypes and number of Special Attribute Points (for Edge, Magic, or Resonance).
- **Special Attribute Points** may only be spent on Edge, Magic, or Resonance — never on Mental/Physical attributes.
- Unspent Special Attribute Points disappear after character creation.
- Characters begin at their metatype's starting attribute values (the number before the slash in the Metatype Attribute Table).
- Max attribute rating for most metatypes: Magic 6, Resonance 6, Edge 6 (humans Edge 7).

> **Cross-reference:** Metatype Attribute Table (p.66) for racial base/max values, racial bonuses/disadvantages.

### Mental and Physical Attributes

- **Attribute column** determines total points for Mental + Physical attributes.
- 1 point raises an attribute by 1.
- Must spend ALL attribute points during creation.
- Cannot spend attribute points on Special Attributes (Edge, Magic, Resonance).
- At character creation, only ONE Physical or Mental attribute may be at the natural maximum limit. Exception: the Exceptional Attribute quality allows a second.
- This limit does not apply to Edge, Magic, or Resonance.

### Step 3: Choose Magic or Resonance

Priority Levels A–C grant a starting Magic or Resonance rating plus free skills/spells/complex forms. Priority D gives reduced magic. Priority E gives none.

**Magic User Types:**

- **Adept:** Channel mana into body. Get Power Points = Magic rating. Cannot astrally project. Can perceive astral only with Astral Perception adept power. Cannot use Magic skill groups (Sorcery, Conjuring, Enchanting).
- **Magician:** Can astrally perceive and project. Can cast spells, conjure spirits, enchant. Can choose freely from all Magic skill groups. Max formulae per group at creation = Magic Rating x 2.
- **Aspected Magician:** Limited magician. Can astrally perceive but NOT project. Must choose ONE Magic skill group (Sorcery, Conjuring, or Enchanting) — locked out of the other two permanently. Can only learn Counterspelling if they are a spellcaster. Same formulae limit as magicians.
- **Mystic Adept:** Combination of magician and adept. Can NEVER astrally project. Can astrally perceive only with Astral Perception adept power. Purchase spells/rituals/preparations like magicians. Must buy Power Points with Karma (5 Karma per Power Point), max points = Magic attribute. Can use Enchanting, Sorcery, or Conjuring skill groups.
- **Technomancer:** Uses Resonance instead of Magic. Gets complex forms and Resonance-based skills (Compiling, Decompiling, Registering).

> **Ambiguity:** Mystic adepts never astrally project per the rules, but the Astral Perception adept power availability is not explicitly stated in this section — it's referenced at p.312.

### Step 4: Purchase Qualities

- Characters start with 25 Karma.
- **Positive Qualities** cost Karma (subtracted from the 25).
- **Negative Qualities** grant bonus Karma (added to the pool).
- Max at creation: 25 Karma worth of Positive Qualities, 25 Karma worth of Negative Qualities.
- After creation: buy Positive Qualities at listed Karma cost x 2. Buy off Negative Qualities at listed bonus Karma x 2.

> **Cross-reference:** Full quality listings on p.71–87. Quality summary tables on p.73.

### Step 5: Purchase Skills

- **Skills column** shows two numbers: individual skill points / skill group points.
- 1 skill point = 1 rank in a skill OR 1 new skill at rank 1.
- Skill groups must be purchased at the same rating for all skills in the group.
- Max skill rating at creation: 6 (7 with Aptitude quality).
- **Specializations:** Cost 1 skill point. Grants +2 dice pool bonus in that area. No limit on specializations per skill, but can only buy one at creation per skill. Cannot buy a specialization for a skill group (breaks the group).
- **Knowledge and Language Skills:** Free points = (Intuition + Logic) x 2. One native language at no cost. Max language/knowledge skill rating: 6.
- Knowledge skill categories: Academic (Logic), Interests (Intuition), Professional (Logic), Street (Intuition).

### Step 6: Spend Your Resources

- **Resources column** determines starting nuyen for gear.
- May convert up to 10 Karma to nuyen at 2,000 nuyen per Karma point (max 20,000 extra nuyen).
- **Three restrictions on gear at creation:**
  1. Each augmentation attribute bonus (Mental and Physical) capped at +4. If attribute hasn't reached natural max, can still raise naturally with Karma, but augmentation + natural can never exceed +4 bonus cap.
  2. Max Availability: 12. Max Device Rating: 6.
  3. All gear subject to GM approval.
- Only standard and alphaware grades available at creation (not betaware/deltaware).
- Augmentations that replace racial features remove the racial bonus (e.g., cybereyes on an elf remove low-light vision).
- Augmentation notation: natural rating first, augmented in parentheses — e.g., Strength 4 (6).
- **Essence:** Any fraction of Essence loss reduces Magic or Resonance by 1. Round Essence UP to nearest whole number before calculating Social limit.

**Lifestyle:** Street (Free), Squatter (500), Low (2,000), Middle (5,000), High (10,000), Luxury (100,000).

**Starting Nuyen:** Roll dice per lifestyle level. Max 5,000 nuyen carryover from unspent creation funds.

**Cyberware/Bioware:** Four grades — standard, alphaware, betaware, deltaware. Only standard and alphaware at creation. Cyberware costs more Essence than bioware. Magic users/technomancers risk losing Magic/Resonance to 0 from too many augmentations.

### Step 7: Spending Your Leftover Karma

Remaining Karma can be spent on:

- Raising attributes/skills (per Karma Advancement Tables, p.107)
- Bound Spirits: 1 Karma per service (Force = Magic attribute). Max bound spirits = Charisma.
- Complex Forms: 4 Karma each. Max at creation = Logic attribute.
- Spells: 5 Karma each. Max at creation = Magic Rating x 2.
- Bond Foci: Variable (see p.318). Max total Force of bonded foci at creation = Magic Rating x 2.
- **Contacts:** 1 Karma per Connection point + 1 Karma per Loyalty point. Min 2 Karma per contact (min 1 Connection, min 1 Loyalty). Max 7 Karma per single contact at creation. Free Karma for contacts = Charisma x 3.
- Registering Sprites: 1 Karma per task (Level = Resonance Rating). Max registered sprites = Charisma.
- Max Karma carryover into play: 7.
- One attribute limit still applies. Max Availability 12, Device Rating 6 still apply.

> **Cross-reference:** Character Advancement section (p.103) for Karma spending on attributes/skills.

### Step 8: Final Calculations

Derived statistics calculated from final attribute values:

**Initiative:**

- Physical: (Intuition + Reaction) + 1D6. Augmentations can add attribute bonuses and extra Initiative Dice.
- Astral: (Intuition x 2) + 2D6
- Matrix AR: (Intuition + Reaction) + 1D6
- Matrix VR Cold-Sim: (Data Processing + Intuition) + 3D6
- Matrix VR Hot-Sim: (Data Processing + Intuition) + 4D6

**Inherent Limits:**

- Mental: [(Logic x 2) + Intuition + Willpower] / 3 (round up)
- Physical: [(Strength x 2) + Body + Reaction] / 3 (round up)
- Social: [(Charisma x 2) + Willpower + Essence] / 3 (round up). Round Essence UP before calculating.

**Condition Monitor:**

- Physical: [Body / 2] + 8 (round up). Add bonuses to Body before calculating.
- Stun: [Willpower / 2] + 8 (round up). Add bonuses to Willpower before calculating.
- Overflow: Body + Augmentation bonuses

**Living Persona (Technomancers only):**

- Attack = Charisma, Data Processing = Logic, Device Rating = Resonance, Firewall = Willpower, Sleaze = Intuition

**Reputation:** Notoriety, Public Awareness, Street Cred (all start at 0 unless modified by qualities like Bad Rep).

### Step 9: Final Touches

Finalize background/backstory and get GM approval. No mechanical rules.

### Alternate Gameplay Levels

**Street-Level Play:**

- Resources: A=75,000, B=50,000, C=25,000, D=15,000, E=6,000
- 13 Karma (max 26 total with Negative Qualities)
- Max Device Rating: 4, Max Availability: 10
- Max Karma-to-nuyen conversion: 5 Karma (10,000 nuyen)

**Prime Runner Play:**

- Resources: A=500,000, B=325,000, C=210,000, D=150,000, E=100,000
- 35 Karma (max 70 total with Negative Qualities)
- Max Device Rating: 6, Max Availability: 15
- Max Karma-to-nuyen conversion: 25 Karma (50,000 nuyen)
- Contact Karma pool: Charisma x 6 (instead of x 3)

### Character Advancement

**Karma Costs:**

- Improve attribute: New Rating x 5 Karma
- Improve Active Skill: New Rating x 2 Karma
- Improve Active Skill Group: New Rating x 5 Karma
- Improve Knowledge/Language Skill: New Rating x 1 Karma
- New specialization: 7 Karma
- New Knowledge/Language Skill: 1 Karma
- New Positive Quality: Karma cost x 2
- Remove Negative Quality: Bonus Karma value x 2
- New complex form: 4 Karma
- New initiate level: 10 + (Grade x 3) Karma
- New spell: 5 Karma

**Training Time:**

- Attribute (Mental, Physical, Magic, Resonance): New Rating x 1 week
- Edge: No time required
- Skill Rating 1–4: New Rating x 1 day
- Skill Rating 5–8: New Rating x 1 week
- Skill Rating 9–13: New Rating x 2 weeks
- Skill Group: New Rating x 2 weeks
- New Specialization: 1 month
- Instructor reduces training time by 25%
- Dependents quality increases training time by 50%
- Max attribute improvement per downtime: 2 ratings
- Max Active/Knowledge/Language skill improvement per downtime: 3 rating points
- Only 1 Mental + 1 Physical attribute, OR 1 attribute + 1 skill, per downtime period
- Cannot improve attribute during augmentation recovery downtime

> **Ambiguity:** The text says a character may raise Active, Knowledge, or Language skills "by a maximum of 3 rating points per any one downtime," but it's unclear whether this is per-skill or total across all skills.

> **Cross-reference:** Using Instruction (p.141) for instructor rules. Magic Loss (p.278), Resonance Loss (p.249) for augmentation impact.

## Implementation Notes

- **Processing order matters:** Priority assignment must complete before any other step. Attributes depend on metatype selection. Magic/Resonance depends on priority. Skills depend on attributes (for Knowledge skill free points). Gear depends on resources + Karma conversion. Final calculations depend on everything else.
- **Suggested step enforcement order:** Priority > Metatype > Attributes > Magic/Resonance > Qualities > Skills > Resources/Gear > Karma Spending > Final Calculations.
- **Karma tracking:** Maintain a running Karma ledger. Start at 25, subtract Positive Quality costs, add Negative Quality bonuses, subtract any Karma spent on skills/attributes/contacts/spells/etc. Display remaining throughout.
- **Augmentation tracking:** Track natural vs augmented attribute values separately. Display as "Natural (Augmented)" format, e.g., "4 (6)". Essence tracking must be fractional (not integer).
- **Gameplay level:** The alternate gameplay levels (Street, Prime) modify Resources amounts, Karma pools, max Availability, max Device Rating, and contact Karma. Implement as a selectable mode that adjusts these constraints globally.
- **Quality interactions:** Several qualities have mutual exclusions (Lucky vs Exceptional Attribute, Blandness vs Distinctive Style, Natural Immunity vs Weak Immune System). Some qualities modify the character creation process itself (Aptitude raises skill cap, Exceptional Attribute raises attribute cap).
- **Edge cases:** Troll lifestyle costs are +100%, Dwarf +20%. Ork Poser limited to humans and elves. Elf Poser limited to humans. Bilingual only at creation. Aspected magicians can never gain access to other Magic skill groups.
