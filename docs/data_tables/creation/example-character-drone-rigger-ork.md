# Drone Rigger - Ork

An ork rigger specializing in drone operations with an impressive fleet of aerial, ground, and walker drones. This character operates as a one-person army, controlling multiple armed drones while maintaining a vehicle for transport. The Juryrigger quality allows improvised repairs in the field.

### Tactical Capabilities

- **Surveillance Network:** 6 surveillance drones provide comprehensive recon coverage
- **Air Support:** Two Roto-Drones offer mobile firepower (lethal and non-lethal options)
- **Ground Assault:** Doberman and Steel Lynx provide heavy weapons platform
- **Command Vehicle:** Rover 2072 serves as mobile base with its own weapons

### Key Bonuses

- Control Rig 2: +2 dice, +2 limits when jumped in
- Pilot Aircraft/Ground 6: Base 6 dice for drone operation
- Gunnery 5: Strong weapons operation
- Juryrigger: +2 dice for field repairs, reduced threshold
- Automotive Mechanic Facility: Full repair capability at home

### Operational Notes

- Hot-sim VR provides 4D6 initiative for rapid drone control
- Can operate multiple drones simultaneously using RCC (if equipped)
- Heavy essence loss (3.2 remaining) limits future augmentation
- Low Charisma (2) makes character poorly suited for face work
- Obscure lifestyle location helps protect drone fleet
