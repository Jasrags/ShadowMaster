## Notes

- **Target**: The target type for the complex form (Persona, Device, File, Host, IC, Sprite, Self)
- **Duration**: 
  - **S** = Sustained (lasts as long as you keep it going)
  - **I** = Instantaneous (effect happens and then fades)
  - **P** = Permanent (effect becomes lasting after sustaining for Force Combat Turns)
  - **E** = Extended (with parameters, e.g., "E (5, 24 hours)")
- **Fading**: The fading value formula based on Level (L). Formulas like L-2, L-1, L+1, L-3, L-4, L-5, L-6 indicate the fading value calculation. Some complex forms (like Coriolis) may not have a fading value listed.
- **Source**: Source book reference (SR5 = Core Rulebook)
